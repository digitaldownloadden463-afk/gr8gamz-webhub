GR8 GAMZ V32 upload instructions

1. Download and unzip the V32 update pack.
2. Open github.com/digitaldownloaden463-afk/gr8gamz-webhub
3. Make sure branch is main.
4. Click Add file > Upload files.
5. Drag the CONTENTS of the unzipped folder, not the outer folder.
6. Correct paths should look like:
   src/app/live/page.js
   src/components/passport/ArcadePulsePanel.js
   src/components/community/ClubhouseSubmitForm.js
   src/app/community/[room]/page.js
   src/data/community.js
7. Commit message:
   Add V32 Arcade Pulse missions and Clubhouse action layer
8. Wait for Vercel to deploy.
9. Test:
   https://www.gr8gamz.com/live
   https://www.gr8gamz.com/daily-challenge
   https://www.gr8gamz.com/community/game-requests
   https://www.gr8gamz.com/community/high-scores
   https://www.gr8gamz.com/community/bug-reports
   https://www.gr8gamz.com/updates/v32-arcade-pulse-missions-clubhouse-action-layer
