# GR8 GAMZ V4 Premium Gameplay Pass

This update upgrades the first two flagship games into the new GR8 GAMZ premium standard.

## Games upgraded

### Neon Snake Rush V4
- Premium start screen with objective, mobile controls, desktop controls and pro tip.
- Cinematic neon grid arena.
- Glowing segmented snake.
- Animated energy orb with pulse effects.
- Particle burst on collection.
- Combo timer and combo multiplier.
- Speed ramp every few energy pickups.
- New-best celebration.
- Impactful game-over screen with score, best, max combo and energy collected.
- Swipe controls, large touch arrows, WASD/arrow keys and instant restart.

### Turbo Drift Grid V4
- Premium start screen with racing objective and touch instructions.
- Cinematic neon racing track with glow, grid movement and chevrons.
- Improved car art with underglow, taillights, engine trails and boost effect.
- Barriers, boost orbs and particle bursts.
- Distance scoring, near-miss bonuses and boost count.
- Speed ramps over time.
- Impactful crash/game-over screen with distance, best, near misses and boosts.
- Hold left/right mobile controls, A/D or arrow keys on desktop and instant restart.
