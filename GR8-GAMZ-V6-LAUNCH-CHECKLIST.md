- Extra mobile polish for smaller screens

## Manual launch checks

Test on a real phone first:

1. Open `/`
2. Open `/games`
3. Open each game page
4. Confirm the game starts quickly
5. Confirm touch controls work
6. Confirm game-over / restart is obvious
7. Confirm ad boxes are not near controls
8. Open `/sitemap.xml`
9. Open `/robots.txt`
10. Open `/ads.txt`
